HOSTEL FEE IN JNTUK

The hostel fee at JNTUK is approximately 10,000/- to 12,000/- per year. Each room in the hostel is allocated to three members, and basic amenities such as beds, almirahs, and study tables are provided. The hostel also offers wifi connectivity for students' convenience.
